Future Camps

What To Expect.

Our upcoming specialized camps are high-intensity, multi-day development programs. They pack a season's worth of advanced instruction, high-repetition drill work, and competitive game-like scenarios into an immersive environment.

Elite Catching Camps

"Mastering the Dish." Entirely focused on the unique physical and mental demands of the toughest position on the field.

  • Mechanical Progressions: receiving, blocking, transfers.
  • Footwork & Agility Circuits: shave milliseconds off pop times.
  • Game-Calling: pitch sequencing, reading hitters, managing pitchers.
  • Live In-Camp Bullpens under Coach's direct mentoring.

Hitting Camps

"The Complete Dynamic Swinger." Break down mechanics to build powerful, consistent, and highly adaptable offensive players.

  • Movement Diagnostics: in-person analysis of setup, load, bat path.
  • Blast Motion Stations: real-time data on bat & hand speed.
  • Targeted Drill Progressions: heavy balls, short bats, tee work.
  • Situational Hitting: two-strike at-bats, off-speed adjustments.
  • Take-Home Blueprint: personalized drills for independent work.

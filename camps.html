Summer 2026 · Week-Long Programs

TI Baseball Summer Camps.

Build confidence, improve fundamental baseball skills, and have fun in a positive, high-energy environment. Our week-long programs are designed to help players develop their hitting, throwing, fielding, baserunning, and game awareness through engaging drills, skill stations, and game-based activities.

Led by experienced coaches, each week emphasizes quality instruction, individual skill development, and a love for the game — ensuring every player leaves more confident and excited to play baseball.

Come Prepared

What Players Should Bring.

  • Baseball glove
    Broken in if possible
  • Bat
    Wood, aluminum, or composite
  • Hat / Cap
    Sun & sweat protection
  • Batting helmet
    With or without face guard
  • Running shoes
    Turf or sneakers — no cleats indoors
  • Water bottle
    Full — refill stations on site

A snack is recommended for the mid-session break. Sunscreen if outdoors. Loaner gear is available for first-time players — just let Coach know in the registration form.

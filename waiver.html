Before You Book

Assumption Of Risk & Liability Waiver.

Every athlete training with TI Baseball Academy — or their parent/guardian — acknowledges and agrees to the terms below. By checking the agreement box on the contact form or any Cal.com booking, you accept these terms on behalf of the participant.

I understand that participation in baseball activities provided by TI Baseball Academy, including hitting, throwing, catching, fielding, drills, practices, lessons, camps, clinics, strength and conditioning, and related training activities, involves inherent risks of injury.

These risks may include, but are not limited to, being struck by a baseball, bat, or equipment; slips, trips, falls; collisions; muscle strains; sprains; fractures; concussions; and other injuries that may result in permanent disability or death.

I voluntarily assume all risks associated with participation in TI Baseball Academy programs and activities.

In consideration of being allowed to participate, I release and hold harmless TI Baseball Academy, its owners, coaches, instructors, employees, contractors, and representatives from any and all claims arising from participation in any TI Baseball Academy activity, except where prohibited by law.

I certify that the participant is physically capable of participating in baseball activities and understand that any medical expenses resulting from injury are my responsibility.

How acceptance works

When you tick "I have read and agree to the TI Baseball Academy waiver" on the contact form or any Cal.com booking page, the system records your acceptance with your name, email, date, and time. That record is sent to Coach and stored with your booking.

Coach may also ask the parent/guardian to sign a printed copy of this waiver in person before the first training session. The two are equivalent — whichever you complete satisfies the requirement.

For minors (under 18)
A parent or legal guardian must be the one accepting these terms on the athlete's behalf.

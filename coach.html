Pro Experience · Elite Development

32 Years Of Baseball. Lived From The Inside.

Coach Crosby brings over three decades of high-level baseball expertise to the diamond. With 32 years in the game — including securing a baseball scholarship and playing 3 years of professional baseball — he knows exactly what it takes to reach the highest levels of the sport.

Known simply as "Coach," he uses his extensive background to provide precise, in-person movement analysis for the next generation of hitters, pitchers, and catchers.

"If you are dedicated to mastering the craft, I have the blueprint to get you there."

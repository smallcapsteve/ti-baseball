Our Training Philosophy

Master The Craft.

At Tools of Ignorance, we are dedicated to mastering the craft of baseball. We combine expert, in-person movement analysis with targeted drills to correct mechanics and unlock peak performance. No shortcuts — just hard work, high repetitions, and a gritty commitment to building elite hitters, pitchers, and catchers.

  • Pro-Level Evaluation. Every catch, hit, and pitch analyzed with an expert eye.
  • Scholarship-Ready Standards. Built for college and pro-level skill, durability, and mental toughness.
  • Targeted Drill Work. High-rep customized drills that rewrite muscle memory.
  • Game-Day Habits. Translate practice gains into in-game results.

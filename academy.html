Winter Development Program

TI Baseball Academy

October – April · 6-month high-performance baseball development program — 2 sessions per week with 3 nights available so families can fit it into their schedule. For athletes serious about improving their skills, increasing their baseball IQ, and preparing for the upcoming season.

3 Days / Week

Why The Academy

Six Months. Two Sessions A Week. Built To Maximize Potential.

The TI Baseball Academy Winter Development Program is a comprehensive six-month program providing a structured training environment focused on technical skill development, athletic performance, and game understanding.

Athletes train two days per week in a high-energy, professional setting — and the Academy runs three nights a week, so each athlete picks the two evenings that fit their schedule. Every session is designed to build confidence, consistency, and long-term player development.

  • 6 months, October through April.
  • 2 training days per week (out of 3 available evenings — pick the nights that work for you).
  • Six development tracks — hitting, pitching, catching, defense, athletic performance, and baseball IQ.
  • Individual assessments & video analysis woven into every session.

What's Included

Six Development Tracks.

Every athlete gets focused work across all six tracks — not just the position they play. The result: complete players who can move, hit, throw, think, and lead.

Hitting Development

  • Swing mechanics and efficiency
  • Bat speed and power development
  • Approach and situational hitting
  • Pitch recognition and timing
  • Video analysis and swing breakdowns

Pitching Development

  • Throwing mechanics
  • Velocity and arm care training
  • Pitch design and command
  • Bullpen work and progress tracking
  • Video analysis and mechanical assessments

Catching Development

  • Receiving and framing
  • Blocking and footwork
  • Throwing mechanics and pop times
  • Game management and leadership

Defensive Development

  • Infield fundamentals and advanced play
  • Outfield routes, reads, and throwing mechanics
  • Position-specific training and game situations

Strength, Speed & Athletic Performance

  • Strength and conditioning
  • Speed and acceleration training
  • Agility and movement efficiency
  • Injury prevention and mobility work

Baseball IQ & Classroom Learning

  • Situational awareness and decision-making
  • Offensive and defensive strategies
  • Positioning and game preparation
  • Mental performance and confidence building
  • Classroom sessions on baseball knowledge

Assessment & Progress Tracking

Every Athlete Measured. Every Athlete Improving.

Improvement isn't a vibe — it's data. Each athlete gets a baseline, video documentation of their movements, and a documented plan for their progression through the season.

Technical Evaluations

Movement assessments, mechanics breakdowns, and benchmark testing at the start of the program — and re-tested throughout to track growth.

Video Analysis

Hitting, throwing, and defensive movements captured on video, broken down frame-by-frame, and shared back with the athlete for visual learning.

Individual Development Plans

Each athlete leaves with a personalized roadmap — specific drills, specific milestones, specific work to do between sessions.

Ongoing Progress Monitoring

Re-assessments throughout the six months so athletes — and their parents — can see exactly how much ground they've covered.

Subscription Management

Cancel Or Change A Subscription.

If you signed up for a recurring TI Baseball program — Weekly 1-on-1, Monday Night Monthly, or the Academy — you can cancel or update payment any time. There are two easy ways.

Self-Serve · Fastest

Manage Through Stripe.

Open the secure Stripe customer portal. Enter the email you used at checkout — Stripe will email you a one-time login link. Once you're in, you can cancel, update your card, change billing info, or download past receipts. No account or password to remember.

Open Stripe Portal
Alternative — Email Coach

Or Send Coach An Email.

Prefer a human? Tell Coach the athlete's name and which program you'd like to cancel. He'll confirm by reply and the next charge won't run.

Email Coach

What happens when you cancel

  • You keep access through the end of the billing period you've already paid for.
  • No further charges will run after that.
  • You can re-enroll any time from the Programs page.

Questions Before You Cancel?

Talk To Coach.

Sometimes a quick conversation with Coach about the program is all it takes. Reach out before cancelling — we'd rather work it out with you.

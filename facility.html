Where We Train

Our Facility — B360.

TI Baseball runs all programs out of B360 in Ajax — a 70-foot, three-cage indoor batting facility with professional pitching machines, dedicated turf, and the room to actually do real reps year-round.

Three Lanes. Real Reps.

Built For Baseball.

The cages are designed for players of all ages and skill levels — whether you’re a beginner learning the basics or a competitive athlete sharpening your swing. Adjustable speeds, professional pitching machines, and a safe, well-lit environment make it possible to get serious reps in regardless of the weather or the season.
